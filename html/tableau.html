How are profits faring in the 50 states of the United States?

Here we see a map depicting profit sums by state. Most of the profits are leaning towards New York and California



Now we will pick a state that has high profits (like California) and describe how this state is faring by profit.


According to this line graph showing profits broken down by month over the last two years for California, we can clearly see that not only is it profitable but it is also continually growing in profitability as time goes on.



Now we will examine California's profits by each product category to see what's driving its growth.


As we can see, the office supplies category contribute most of California's profit.



For the most profitable category, we will now forecast it through November 2015 to show expected profits for California's Office Supplies.


From the graph, we can see that the office supply category of California will continue to grow through November 2015.
